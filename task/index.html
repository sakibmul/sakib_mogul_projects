Ocean/ Sea Latitude and Longitude

When inputing the Latitude and Longitude for the Ocean/ Sea location the latutude and longitude must be the coordinates a body of water. If the latutude and longitude coordinates are land based no results will be displayed.

Ocean/ Sea Examples

Latitude: 39. Longitude: -73

Latitude: -41. Longitude: 120

Latitude: 43. Longitude: 34

Latitude: 34. Longitude: 18

Airport ICAO Weather Information

When inputing the ICAO Code for a particular Airport it can be done in upper or lower case. Smaller Airfields and Airports ICAO Codes may not be on the Geonames Database?

ICAO Examples

LSZH

EGLL

LFPG

KJFK

Weather Information Latitude and Longitude

When inputing the Latitude and Longitude for the Weather Infomation the latitude and longitude have to be based on land, if the coordinates inputed are Ocean or Sea based no results would be returned.

Weather Info Examples

Latitude: 52. Longitude: -0.7

Latitude: 43. Longitude: 2

Latitude: 39. Longitude: -77

Latitude: -35. Longitude: 149
